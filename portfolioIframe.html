Dance, alongside with contemporary forms of art and philosophy, has undergone a transformation in the fundamental cosmological approach to its craft. No longer is it about representing the unity of a harmonic order of the perfect forms of the cosmos. Contemporary discourse has challenged the ability for individual subjects to be able to obtain true knowledge of this underlying unity if it exists at all. Individual perspectives are the focus of investigations and dance has become about the interpretations that each audience member has to the performance at the stage. There is no longer a proper way to undergo the experience the art of dance in contemporary functions because the dance becomes about the relationship of the choreography to the subject observing it.
